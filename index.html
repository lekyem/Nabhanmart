Nabhan Sales Dashboard

I designed and developed a dynamic Excel Sales Dashboard tailored for a leading supermarket, offering real-time insights into their sales performance. This interactive tool consolidates sales data from multiple sources, providing a comprehensive overview of daily, weekly, and monthly sales trends.

Key Features:

  • Real-time sales data visualization
  • Sales trends analysis
  • Product performance tracking
  • Interactive filters for date and product categories
  • User-friendly design for easy navigation

As the primary creator of this dashboard, I meticulously structured the Excel workbook, applied advanced data analysis techniques, and designed an intuitive user interface. The dashboard has empowered the supermarket's management team to make data-driven decisions, optimize product offerings, and enhance overall sales strategies.

The project involved data collection, data cleaning, and the creation of pivot tables, charts, and slicers to ensure data accuracy and accessibility. This Excel Sales Dashboard has significantly improved the supermarket's sales tracking and reporting capabilities, resulting in enhanced profitability and strategic growth.

Nabhan Sales Distribution Dashboard

As the creator of this dashboard, I harnessed geospatial data and leveraged data analytics to develop a powerful tool for optimizing sales distribution. This dashboard equips businesses with the ability to make informed decisions on inventory allocation, expansion opportunities, and market focus.

The project involved data integration from various sources, geospatial mapping, and the development of interactive features for user-friendly navigation. The Sales Distribution Dashboard has significantly contributed to enhancing market coverage and improving overall sales strategies for businesses.
